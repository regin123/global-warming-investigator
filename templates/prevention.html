Prevention
So, what can you do to help reduce CO2 emission? How can you change your life so that the planet would have bigger chances to survive?
Choose a bicycle over a car
{% load static %}
Average CO2 emission for diesel cars is equal to 122,3 g/km. Just choosing bicycle on a 5 km way to uni every day reduces emission of CO2 of about 165 kg in a year.
Avoid flying by planes
{% load static %}
Average CO2 emission for plane is around 90 km per passenger per hour. If you resign from two trips a year travelling by plane on an average flight, you will reduce the emission of CO2 by 3 tones a year!
